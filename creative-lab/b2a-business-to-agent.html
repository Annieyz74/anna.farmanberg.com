B2A: Business to Agent

9 June 2026

We've built business on human relationships for decades. But what happens when your customer is an agent?

B2C, B2B, B2B2C — and now also B2A: Business to Agent.

People browse, read reviews, talk to other people and trust their feelings when making decisions. The thing with AI agents is: they don't. They analyze, compare, decide and buy on behalf of someone else. And suddenly, a lot of what we've relied on starts to shift.

The relationships, the trust, the recommendations and the conversations we've had. How much of that still matters when the one making the decision is a machine?

Remember, agents can't choose you if they can't find you.

We've spent years optimizing for human eyes: clean design, inspirational UX, storytelling and brand voice. Now there's a second audience around — one that reads data, not feelings. And honestly, that changes things.

That means structure, definition and consistency matter more than ever before. Because agents read what's structured and ignore what isn't.

What does it mean for you?

Here's where it gets trickier, because it depends on your business, your industry and your customer journey.

But a good starting point could be to ask yourself a simple question:

Is what you put out there clear, consistent and machine‑readable?

Because visibility used to be about humans — now it's also about agents.

Are you visible to agents today? If not, when do you start?
