How I work in practice

Website Design Project

I approached my personal website as a design challenge. I wanted to understand the fundamentals, so I built it from scratch together with Claude and Copilot instead of relying on templates. I started with a simple MVP, tested layouts and iterated until the structure felt clean and purposeful. I focused on usability, personal brand and a professional tone — and removed anything that didn't add value.

I also asked a few people for constructive feedback and used it to refine the site further. The result is a functional, minimal site that reflects how I think and work.

Startup Experience — Working in Sync to Move Forward

At an early‑stage startup, I worked with founders who wanted to enter the corporate travel space — a far more complex industry than they expected. I started with a structured assessment, looking at their potential, risks and readiness with an investor‑like lens. From there, I brought in the right teams, created alignment and supported them through the steep learning curve.

We moved forward together, step by step, and they eventually grew into a well‑known agency. The experience reinforced how I work: understand the reality, meet people where they are, and help new players succeed in demanding environments.
