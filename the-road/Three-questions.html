Three Questions That Keep Me on Track

7 May 2026

Are you focusing on what you should be doing right now? I believe we all think we do most of the time — but honestly? Work life is busy, full of constant interruptions and complex environments.

So how do we stay focused and make sure we're doing the right things?

Three questions I ask myself

In my case, I come back to three questions that help me stay on track:

  • ·Does it feel right?
  • ·Does this move me forward?
  • ·Am I being honest right now?

What they connect to

For me, these questions connect to something deeper:

Feeling = values and integrity.
It's about staying aligned with what I stand for.

Forward = strategy and direction.
A reminder to choose actions that actually move me toward my goals.

Honesty = transparency and accountability.
Being honest with myself about why I'm doing something and what truly matters.

I'd love to hear your tips — how do you make sure you stay on track?
