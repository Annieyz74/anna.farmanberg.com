The Sticker on the Lens Box — And What It Says About Customer Experience

12 May 2026

Think about a brand you like. Are you loyal to that brand — or the store you buy it from? Or both? Perhaps it depends on what you're buying, the price or quality.

Regardless of whether it's B2B or B2C — customers love the "no brainers." It should be simple and effortless to make the purchase.

The sticker I hadn't seen before

I was looking at my new lens box and noticed a sticker I hadn't seen before. The company name, a QR code and Scan to reload. I smiled. This is nudging — making it effortless to place my next order.

What are the odds for me to buy elsewhere now?

So simple — and making it a no brainer to scan for the next order. I assume they will closely follow the sticker's data. Sales increase, retention, NPS scores and so on.

How come I haven't seen this more often from other companies?

Walking in your customer's shoes

The customer journey is about understanding the customer — walking in their shoes. The importance of understanding your customers might sound like a cliché. But it's not.

It's where you should invest your time — close potential gaps and mitigate the risks.

What could be your sticker on the lens box right now?
